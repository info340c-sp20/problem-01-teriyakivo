Zuko

Zuko is a fire bending master from the show Avatar: the Last Airbender . He is son to Firelord Ozai, brother to Princess Azula and nephew to General Iroh. Zuko is arguably the most dynamic character in the series. He transforms from an angsty teenage boy looking to redeem his honor to a compassionate, righteous mentor to the person who he was formerly seeking to capture.

Reasons why I admire Zuko:

  • He fights for his honor.
  • He has a cool scar on his face.
  • His uncle Iroh is OP.
